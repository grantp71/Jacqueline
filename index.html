About Us

Jacqueline | Antiques was created as a way for our family to VALUE THE PASSED. Not only items that pass through generations but we also value the passage of time.

Services

VALUE THE PASSED

Jacqueline | Antiques provides a digital platform allowing anyone to enjoy the antique world. Tribal Native, Eastern Colonial, Westward Expansion, War of the States, Industrial Age, Depression Era, and Modern all are found in the States. The familiar works of Europe and finds from Near East and Far..then explore the Southern Hemisphere spanning continents and time..

Auction

We advise care when one wishes for something because we would very much like to make that wish come true. With an adhearance to house rules from Christy's but at a pace slowed to that of eBay, our online auctions may be set with reserves to meet and time to consider. Or not. We love the hunt and will hold live auctions..

Appraisals

Without a doubt the most challenging aspect of any type of trade..What is it worth? Sharing data using today's technology allows a data-driven specificity that the antiquarian field has never seen. So, beware of fakes, but let us have Google, Bing, Kovel's and The Smithsonian Institue lend us a hand..

Consignments

A picture could be worth a single BitCoin. Our step-by-step guide to shooting the best photos with the phone in your hand will allow us to survey your piece and facilitate a purchase.

Sales

Through partnerships with trusted payment options would be the only currency with which Jacqueline|Antiques would operate. Square is our preferred point-of-sale because the company founder, Jim McKelvey, is the founder of another venture called LaunchCode. Ask me about it someday..
